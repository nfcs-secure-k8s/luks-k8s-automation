Background and Context#

Trusted Research Environment and Storage Risk#

Protection against storage backend compromise or theft:#

  • The primary reason for storage encryption is the protection of sensitive data from unauthorized access in the event where the storage is compromised or outrightly stolen. When a storage is compromised or stolen, the encrypted files are unreadable without the decryption key, thereby preventing data breaches (Red Hat, 2023).

Mitigating Risks of Insecure Disposal:#

  • Disposed storages that have reached their end-of-life without proper data erasure poses a significant risk if these storages are not encrypted before disposal.

Regulatory Requirements:#

  • Encryption ensures that researchers working with highly regulated data (healthcare, financial records) are protected from unauthorized use and compliant with regulatory requirements like GDPR, ISO 27001.
